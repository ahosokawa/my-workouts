Install on your phone

This is a web app you install directly from your browser — no app store needed. Follow the steps for your device, then launch it from your home screen like any other app.

iOS
Using Safari
  1. Open ahosokawa.github.io/my-workouts in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Tap Add
Android
Using Chrome
  1. Open ahosokawa.github.io/my-workouts in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu at the top right
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen or Install App
  4. Tap Add

Features

Everything you need for 5/3/1

Auto-calculated sets
Warmup, working, and supplemental sets calculated from your training maxes
Cycle tracking
3-week cycles across Squat, Bench, Deadlift, and OHP with automatic TM progression
AMRAP logging
Log your AMRAP sets and see estimated 1RM via Brzycki formula
PR charts
Visualize your estimated 1RM progression over time for every lift
Rest timer
Built-in timer so you can focus on your workout, not your phone clock
Plate calculator
See exactly which plates to load on each side of the bar
